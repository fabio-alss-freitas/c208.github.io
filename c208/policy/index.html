Privacy Policy for C208

Effective Date: January 1, 2020

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which C208 ("us", "we", or "our") collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User") of the C208 mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information from Users, with their consent:

  • Ad Identifier: Upon the user's authorization during the first start of the app, we collect the advertising identifier associated with their device.
  • Approximated Local Position: With the user's consent, we collect approximate location data to improve support and development activities.

2. Use of Collected Information

We collect the ad identifier to offer personalized advertisements within the App. The approximate local position is utilized to enhance our support and development efforts.

3. Sharing of Collected Information

If authorized by the user, we may share the collected ad identifier and approximate local position with third-party entities for advertising purposes.

4. User Consent

During the first start of the app, a popup will be presented, requesting the user's consent to collect the ad identifier and approximate local position.

5. Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of user data. We implement industry-standard measures to protect against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse of information. We utilize encryption tools and comply with data security requirements mandated by relevant app stores.

6. Data Retention

We retain the collected ad identifier for a period of 6 months for analytics purposes. It is important to note that we do not collect personal information such as names or email addresses.

7. Data Deletion

Upon uninstallation of the app, all collected information is deleted from our systems.

8. Children's Privacy

C208 is not directed towards children and does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 13. If you believe we have unintentionally collected information from a child, please contact us using the information provided in Section 11.

9. Policy Updates

We do not currently have any updates planned for the privacy policy. However, in the event of any future updates, users will be informed via email.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the practices of the C208 app, please contact us at fresansoftware@gmail.com.
